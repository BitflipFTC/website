FIRST Information

FIRST is a worldwide nonprofit organization that provides opportunities for students to compete in team robotics competitions. It empowers people ages 4-18 to design and build their own robots, plan outreach opportunities, request funding from sponsors, compete in tournaments, learn public speaking skills and problem solving, and participate in tournaments. FIRST also gives participants the ability to learn more about career opportunities in the field of STEM, which will aid them in seeking out a job in this field.
