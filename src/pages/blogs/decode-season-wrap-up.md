We are so grateful to have had the incredible opportunity to compete at the FIRST Championship in Houston! We were able to meet and compete with so many amazing people and teams from around the world, and it was such an amazing experience. Congratulations to 11288 OverClucked Bots, 21087 Velocity, and 30030 Exodus for winning the championship! As the season comes to a close, we want to give a huge shoutout to all of the people who have made our team possible! Thank you so much to our sponsors, who include friends, family, and businesses/grants. We also would like to thank the amazing teams we have been able to interact with and learn from throughout the season. Thank you so much to 772 Golden Dragons for letting us use your conference room, reprinting our parts at States so we could continue to compete and qualify for the FIRST Championship, and giving us feedback on our engineering portfolio! Thank you also to 11444 Garnet Squadron for helping us with the design aspect of our robot! You guys are the best! Thank you to 25068 Raptoria for collaborating with us in preparation for States, and to 25209 FRITS for taking the time to meet with us virtually! Thank you to all of the amazing teams that we have had the pleasure to interact with on Discord as well. You are all so amazing and we couldn’t have done it without you! Last but not least, we would like to give a huge thank you to our Coach Erik and to our moms for facilitating our practices, making meals, and helping us create our team! Thank you again to everyone, and we look forward to what the next chapter holds!
